During my internship, I had the opportunity to attend an event that left a lasting impression. It was a journey filled with cutting-edge knowledge, valuable insights, amazing gifts, and truly memorable moments.

### Event 1
* **Event Name:** AWS First Cloud AI Journey Community Day 2026.
* **Time:** 09:00, March 21, 2026.
* **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City.
* **Role:** Attendee.
* **Main Activities:**
    * Analyzing the current state of students and practical demands from the labor market (lack of soft skills, etc.).
    * Sharing the challenges of building platforms (Build Platform) and the trends of migrating to other platforms.
    * Discussing the applications of **Generative AI** in **DevOps** and related supporting tools.
    * Introducing the open-source **Langfuse** (integrated on AWS) to help development teams monitor, evaluate, and debug GenAI applications.
    * Instructions on writing optimized Map Repositories for AI.
* **Results or Values Gained:**
    * Updated on AI trends in DevOps and gained a deeper understanding of how Generative AI applications operate through Langfuse.
    * Understood the gap between academic knowledge and actual market requirements to develop a suitable learning roadmap.
    * Understood the platform deployment process with a product-oriented mindset towards Backend for Frontends.
    * Received valuable lessons from experts and had wonderful networking moments with the AWS community.
